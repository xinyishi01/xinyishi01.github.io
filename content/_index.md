I am Xinyi Shi (<span style="font-family: 'KaiTi'; font-size: 20px; text-align: left; display: inline-block;">石昕怡</span>), a Ph.D. candidate advised by Prof. [Peihan Miao](https://sites.google.com/view/peihanmiao) at Brown University. My research lies in cryptography, with a focus on secure multi-party computation. I received my bachelor degree from ACM Honors Class at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

Outside of research, I am passionate about algorithms and have enjoyed competitive programming. I have participated since high school and earned gold medals in [ICPC](https://icpc.global) and [CCPC](https://ccpc.io) during undergrad.
