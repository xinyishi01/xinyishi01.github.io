About Me

I am Xinyi Shi (石昕怡), a second year Ph.D. student advised by Prof. Peihan Miao at Brown University. Before that, I received my bachelor degree from ACM Honors Class at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My research lies in cryptography, with a focus on secure multi-party computation.
